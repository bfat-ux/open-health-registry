Nigeria

Nigeria Pharmacy Registry

Active

A national registry of licensed pharmacy dispensing endpoints — pharmacies, patent medicine vendors, and hospital dispensaries — across all 36 states and the FCT. Built in coordination with the Pharmacists Council of Nigeria (PCN). FHIR R4-compliant.

How a new program starts

OHR initiates programs in response to a clear regulatory gap and an identified partner — typically a national regulatory authority or ministry of health. The starting conditions are:

  • A defined registry scope (who or what is being registered)
  • A regulatory authority willing to engage as the eventual custodian
  • A data source that can seed the initial L0 population
  • A path to progressive verification that doesn't require perfect data at launch

Programs that don't meet these conditions don't start. OHR doesn't build registries speculatively or without a clear line of sight to regulatory adoption.

If you represent a regulatory authority or ministry of health and want to discuss whether a registry program makes sense for your context, reach out.
