Nigeria Pharmacy Registry

A national registry of licensed pharmacy dispensing endpoints — pharmacies, patent medicine vendors, and hospital dispensaries — across all 36 states and the FCT. FHIR R4-compliant, validation-ladder-based.

What it is

The Nigeria Pharmacy Registry (NPR) is OHR's first program. It is a structured, queryable database of every licensed pharmacy dispensing endpoint in Nigeria — community pharmacies, patent medicine vendors, and hospital dispensaries — geolocated and mapped to their regulatory status under the Pharmacists Council of Nigeria (PCN).

The registry exists because Nigerian health software — EMRs, prescription routing systems, supply chain tools — consistently fails at the same point: it can't reliably answer whether a facility exists, where it is, and whether it's authorised to dispense. The NPR is the answer to that question, built to be consumed by any system that speaks FHIR or REST.

What it covers

As of April 2026, the NPR contains 6,891 records spanning all 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory. Records are classified by facility type and assigned a validation level on OHR's five-point ladder (L0–L4). The current population is predominantly L0 — ingested from available data sources, not yet field-verified. Progressive verification is ongoing.

Technical architecture

The NPR runs as a standalone application — a FastAPI service backed by PostgreSQL and PostGIS — separate from this website. It exposes a REST API documented to OpenAPI 3.1 and returns FHIR R4 Location and Organization resources for all endpoints. The live dashboard is accessible at the link below.

This site links to the NPR; it does not absorb it. The NPR codebase, ingestion pipelines, and database are maintained in a separate repository.

Regulatory engagement

OHR initiated formal correspondence with the Pharmacists Council of Nigeria on April 27, 2026. The conversation is in its earliest stage. The NPR was built in anticipation of this engagement; regulatory adoption has not yet been confirmed.

Current status

Honest accounting, April 2026: 6,891 records ingested. Approximately 4 records promoted past L0. No external users yet. PCN conversation initiated. The dashboard is live and queryable; it is not yet officially endorsed or integrated by any regulatory body.

Validation ladder in the NPR

Every NPR record carries a validation level. Here's what each level means in the context of pharmacy verification.

L0
Mapped Pharmacy name and location ingested from a data source. Not independently verified. Most NPR records are currently here.
L1
Contact Confirmed Phone call or SMS to the listed contact number confirmed and logged. Pharmacy is reachable.
L2
Evidence Documented Field visit with GPS coordinates and timestamped photo. Physical premises confirmed at the listed address.
L3
PCN Verified Record matched to PCN registration database. Licence number and registration status confirmed with the regulator.
L4
High-Assurance Reserved for controlled substance dispensing endpoints. Requires in-person audit or biometric confirmation.
